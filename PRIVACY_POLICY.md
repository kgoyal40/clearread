# ClearRead Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

## What ClearRead does

ClearRead is a browser extension that analyses news articles for bias, credibility, and missing context. When you click "Analyse Page", the extension extracts the article text from the page you are viewing and sends it to our backend server for analysis.

## What data is collected

- **Article text**: The text content of the web page you choose to analyse. This is sent to our server, processed, and discarded. It is not stored.
- **Page URL**: Used to identify the news outlet for reliability checks. Not stored.

## What data is NOT collected

- No personal information (name, email, address)
- No browsing history
- No cookies or tracking
- No analytics or telemetry
- No user accounts

## Third-party services

Article analysis is performed using the Anthropic Claude API. Article text is sent to Anthropic for processing and is subject to [Anthropic's usage policy](https://www.anthropic.com/policies). Outlet ratings are checked against the Media Bias/Fact Check database.

## Data retention

ClearRead does not store any user data. Article text and URLs are processed in memory and discarded immediately after the analysis response is returned.
